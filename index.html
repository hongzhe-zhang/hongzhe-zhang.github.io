Bio

Research at the intersection of analytics, platforms, and financial decision-making.

I am an Assistant Professor in Information Systems at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen. My research develops machine learning and optimization methods for financial technology, privacy-preserving AI, recommender systems, and data-driven business decision-making.

I received my Ph.D. in Financial Services Analytics from the University of Delaware, my M.S. in Statistics from Rutgers University, and my B.S. in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Xiamen University.
