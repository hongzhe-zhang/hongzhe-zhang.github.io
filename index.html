I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Information, Technology and Innovation at Antai College of Economics and Management, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. Prior to joining SJTU, I served as an Assistant Professor in Information Systems at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, from 2023 to 2026.

My research addresses important business and societal problems by developing novel and rigorous machine learning methods rather than simply applying existing ones. A typical research project begins by identifying a significant business or societal problem and analyzing its unique characteristics that make it difficult to solve effectively using existing machine learning algorithms. Based on this analysis, a novel machine learning algorithm or method is developed to tackle these challenges, thereby solving the problem effectively and efficiently. Through this work, I seek to make methodological contributions to both Information Systems and machine learning. This stream of research is known as Computational Design Science in the field of Information Systems.
